# Recent Updates

April 5 2025

  - Obsidian Block (crafted from obs. ingots) renamed to `Obsidian Ingot Block` and has a new texture to make it easier to distinguish from `Obsidian Block`

  - Some shops around Spawn, 10k-East, 18k-East, and Public Mines 1 and 2, are being added to the list of profit sharing shops. If your shop is added to the list, be prepared to have 10%% of cash sales contribute to the server money drops.

  - Several Tree-related textures, including wood/plank blocks, are updated.

  - New Mod `Lumber` adds several wood-derived blocks: `Panel`, `Panel Link`, `Cross Planks`, `Braced Panel`, `Tile`
