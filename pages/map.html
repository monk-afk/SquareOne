  • Cardinal Directions are: Z:(North), -Z:(South), X:(East), -X:(West)
  • Image coordinates are the center point of an area 40962 pixels, each pixel represents one node.
    • This means images have an incremental positional boundary of (-/+) 2048
      In other words, image of 0,0 borders on (-/+)Z:2048,(+/-)X:2048
