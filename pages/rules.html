SERVER RULES

KEEP GAMEPLAY AND CHAT RATED E10+

  • Players are responsible for themselves, their items/pets, and their builds/areas!
  • Use protection blocks, runes, wear armor and carry a weapon to defend yourself!
  • PvP is enabled in unprotected areas! The owner of a protected area can damage trespassers!

Contains content that is suitable for ages 10 and over.

  • Cartoon, fantasy, or mild violence
  • Mild to moderate use of profane language
  • Minimal suggestive themes

Do Not:

  • ❌ Make real-life threats, or verbal abuse In-game violence is generally acceptable as long as it's not overly threatening

  • ❌ Share Specific details of your identity Any information which could lead to discovering your personal identity: including social media, city, year and date of birth, etc. Generalizations are typically allowed, use caution with sharing any information.

  • ❌ Evade Curse Filter Cursing is censored. You're allowed to curse, you're not allowed to bypass the chat filter.

  • ❌ Advertize servers of any kind No unsolicited invites or shares to other games, socials, etc. Use private messages to invite your friends without announcing it to everyone.

  • ❌ Spam or flood the chat Verbal diareah is not allowed, use private or faction chat.

  • ❌ Kill-spree or TP/Spawn kill Killing sprees are judged by staff case-by-case. Repeated offenses will typically invoke harsher judgements. Bone camping/guarding, teleport killing, respawn killing, are forbidden and will result in being smitten repeatedly.

  • ❌ Beg for free stuff or privs Asking occasionally if anyone wants to trade, or if anyone can give something you want, is usually allowed. Repeatedly asking for trades, donations, privs, is not allowed. The donation grid in the inventory always has stuff in it.

  • ❌ Misuse protection blocks Misuse can mean, not limited to: protecting large areas of land without building on it; protecting areas in the sky directly above land area (if it draws a shadow, you're too low); protecting too close to someone else's area without their permission; protecting fresh bones; protecting public resources for the sake of squatting.

  • ❌ Act or Speak Adult (18+) topics No dating. No sexually explicit topics.

Everyone is allowed to uphold the rules of the server. If you're not comfortable speaking out against someone, freely contact staff and we will take care of it!

Have Fun, Stay Safe!
